CHALLENGE OVERVIEW

The AFRETEC-UNILAG Innovation-in-Health Challenge unites Nigeria's top university minds to revolutionize healthcare delivery across Africa through technological innovation.

This initiative supports the development of cutting-edge prototypes addressing critical gaps in African healthcare systems. From AI-powered diagnostics to low-cost medical devices, participants are creating solutions with real-world impact.

Key Focus Areas

  • Digital Health & Telemedicine
  • Maternal & Child Health Solutions
  • AI & Medical Data Analytics
  • Affordable Medical Devices
  • Mental Health Innovations

Why This Matters To you

Beyond competition prizes, this challenge creates lasting value for participants and the healthcare ecosystem:

  • Development Funding Top teams receive up to ₦500,000 for Proof of Concept (POC) development
  • Expert Mentorship Guidance from leading healthcare professionals and technologists
  • Implementation Support Pathways to pilot promising solutions with partner institutions
  • Industry Recognition Showcase innovations to potential investors and healthcare providers

AFRETEC-UNILAG Innovation-in-Health Challenge Young Innovators Forum Speakers

Dr. Obichi Obiajunwa

Executive Director, Hutzpa Innovations

Dr. Obichi Obiajunwa is a visionary leader, innovation consultant, and public health strategist with over a decade of experience driving transformative change across Africa. As the founder of the Young Innovation Leaders Fellowship and executive director of Hutzpa Innovations, he empowers individuals and organizations to turn bold ideas into impactful solutions. With expertise spanning innovation strategy, leadership development, AI ethics, and public health, Dr. Obichi collaborates with global partners to foster sustainable growth. Passionate about mentoring future leaders, he is dedicated to shaping a new generation of innovators who redefine possibilities and create lasting impact.

Abimbola Adebakin

Founder/CEO at Advantage Health Africa

Abimbola Adebakin is the Founder/CEO of Advantage Health Africa. Advantage Health Africa creates a platform for collaboration and performance improvement in the health sector, with initial emphasis on retail pharmacy practice in Nigeria.

They are collaborators by nature, and all they do promotes health innovation, capacity development along the value chain in retail pharmacy, and positioning the industry to better serve its target market.

Dr. Niyi Osamiluyi

Founder/CEO, Premier Health Systems (PHS) Consult

Dr. Adeniyi Osamiluyi is the founder/CEO of Premier Medical Systems (PMS) Nigeria Limited, a digital health social enterprise, with the focus of leveraging Information Technology (IT) to improve access of the underserved to healthcare in Africa. PMS specializes in the design, development, deployment and support of solutions in the Healthcare sector.

Folake Owodunni

CEO, Emergency Response Africa

Folake Owodunni is the co-founder and CEO of Emergency Response Africa (ERA), a healthcare technology company that is changing how medical emergencies are managed in Africa, beginning in Nigeria.

ERA saves lives by connecting individuals experiencing a medical emergency to the largest network of Community First Responders, ambulances, and hospitals to deliver care in minutes using technology.

Francis Sani O.

Programme Director, 3MTT Nigeria

Francis Sani is currently serving as the Principal at [MAGIC Fund] and as a Technical Adviser, with a strong background in business development, start-ups, and entrepreneurship

Noteworthy career highlights include holding the position of Honourable Minister of Communications, Innovation, and Digital Economy as a Technical Adviser, and serving in various leadership roles at organizations such as Magic Fund, Helium Health, and Co-creation Hub (CcHUB).

Dr. Mories Atoki

CEO - ABCHealth

Mories Atoki is a Nigerian lawyer and business executive. She was appointed as the CEO at African Business Coalition For Health (ABC Health) where she champions African led Policy discussions on health, innovation and public-private collaborations. She is the current coordinator of the Health Policy Commission of the National Economic Summit Group (NESG)

Dr. Andrew Redfearn

MathWorks Academia Team

Dr. Andrew Redfearn is part of the academic support team at MathWorks. He has extensive experience teaching MATLAB
